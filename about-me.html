I'm Ryan, but you probably already knew that since you found my website. I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio and graduated in 2016 with a bachelors in Communication Technologies with a focus on human-computer interaction from The Ohio State University. In my free time I enjoy building DIY projects, reading, volunteering with Habitat for Humanity, and trying new things.

In 2015 I was accepted into the Disney College Program internship in Walt Disney World working food & beverage at Columbia Harbour House in the Magic Kingdom. Working for Disney was extremely enjoyable.I became friends with many new people from around the world, I learned many valuable skills, I got to take part in several once-in-a-lifetime events, and made countless unforgettable memories. Everyday brought new and unexpected situations, requiring me to make decisions on the go. Working closely with my team, I strived to keep our location efficient while still creating and delivering those magical experiences to thousands of guests every day.

When I first started the internship, I thought working for Disney would be a temporary thing and that I would go back to living in Ohio after the internship ended. I soon realized that I really enjoyed working for Disney and wanted to make a career out of it. I completed a second internship with Disney after I graduated in 2016 working in merchandise in Hollywood Hills in Hollywood Studios, but I enjoyed working there so much, I ended up moving to Florida and working for Disney for the next 7 years. I worked on 8 different different teams across multiple lines of businesses including food & beverage, merchandise and attractions.I started My Adventure Blog at the begining of my first internship to talk about my experience at Disney. If you would like to read more about my time at Disney, click here.

While working for Disney was enjoyable, I have always been interested in programming, especially creating websites. In 2022 I enrolled in Tech Elevator's national live remote java bootcamp where I learned how to develop dynamic web-based software systems using the Java programming language and platform.

Since Tech Elevator I have been contuning to learn new languages such as Python and C#, new frameworks such as React.js and Angular, and exploring things such as AWS and AI fundamentals.
